Bangalore

Chariot tour, Do's & Dont's, saftey & security etc.
05.30pm to 06.30pm Visit to Chitrakala Parishad / Art Gallery
06.30pm to 07.30pm Cultural Show
08.00pm Arrive at Yeshwanthpur Railway Station, Platform No.6
to a traditional Indian welcome. Dinner on board The Golden Chariot

Chennai
06.00am Arrival in Chennai
08.00am Breakfast on Board The Golden Chariot
09.00am A drive through the Beach Road
09.50am Visit to the famous St. Thomas Church

01.30pm Lunch at GRT Temple Bay

08.00pm Dinner on board the Golden Chariot

Tanjavur
