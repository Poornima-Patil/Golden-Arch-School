Pride of South

Day 1

Monday - Bangalore
03.00 pm to 4.00 pm: Check-in at Taj West End
04.00 pm to 5.00 pm: Nature Walk
05.00 pm to 5.30 pm: Hi-Tea
05.30 pm to 6.00 pm: Presentation on The Golden Chariot Tour
06.00 pm to 6.30 pm: Cultural Show
06.45 pm to 8.00 pm: Visit the ISKCON Temple (The International Society for Krishna Consciousness)
08.15 pm: Arrive at Yeshwanthpur Railway Station, Platform No. 6 to a Traditional Indian Welcome
Dinner onboard The Golden Chariot
9.00 pm: Depart to Mysore

Day 2

Tuesday - Mysore / Kabini/ Bandipur
08.00 am to 09.30 am: Breakfast onboard
10.00 am to 11.00 am: Visit the famous Mysore Palace
11.15 am: Later depart for Kabini
01.15 pm: Reach Jungle Lodges
Lunch at the Jungle Lodges
Post Lunch: Jungle Safari
07:40pm: Screening of wildlife film in the evening
Dinner and stay overnight at Jungle Lodges

Day 3

Wednesday  - Mysore
06:30 am: Boat Ride on the Kabini River
08:00 am: Breakfast at the Jungle Lodges
09:30 am: Depart for Mysore
11:30 am: Back on board The Golden Chariot
01:00 pm: Lunch on board The Golden Chariot
03:30 pm: Mysore sightseeing: A Visit to Srirangapatnam 
07:00 pm: Reach Lalitha Mahal Palace Hotel for a cultural performance followed by dinner.
10:00 pm: Back onboard The Golden Chariot

Day 4

Thursday - Hassan
05:00 am: Depart from Mysore to Hassan
07:30 am: Breakfast onboard
08:15 am: Arrive at Hassan
09:00 am: Depart for sightseeing to Shravanabelagola
(Please note that you would have to climb close to 700 steps to experience the architectural marvel.   Only those who can undertake this are advised to do so. Guests unwilling to climb can stay  back on board The Golden Chariot.)
01:00 pm: Lunch on board The Golden Chariot
02:45 pm: Depart for Belur and Halebidu for sightseeing
07:15 pm: Back on board The Golden Chariot
08:40 pm: Depart to Hospet, Dinner on board The Golden Chariot

Day 5

Friday - Hospet
06:00 am: Arrive at Hospet

 
07:00 am: Breakfast onboard
08:30 am: Depart for Hampi sightseeing: Virupaksha Temple, Local Hampi Bazaar,
Queens's bath, Elephant stables, Royal enclosure, Hazara Rama Temple
01:00 pm: Return to The Golden Chariot for lunch
03:30 pm: Visit to the Vittala Temple with the beautiful Stone Chariot during day time
05.00 pm: Sit out or leisure time by the banks of the Tungabhadra river
07.00 pm: Re-visit the Vittala Temple again for Illumination  
08:00 pm: Back on board The Golden Chariot
Dinner on board The Golden Chariot

Day 6

Saturday - Badami
05:00 am: Depart from Hospet to Badami
07:30 am: Breakfast onboard
08:30 am: Arrive at Badami
08:40 am: Depart after breakfast for sightseeing: Badami Caves and Pattadakal
01:30 pm: Return to The Golden Chariot for lunch
01:40 pm: Depart for Goa
Relax and enjoy the facilities on board - Spa, Gym, Movies, etc.
Dinner on board The Golden Chariot. Reach Goa past midnight

Day 7

Sunday - Goa
08:00 am: Breakfast onboard The Golden Chariot
10:00 am: Depart for sightseeing
11:00 am: Visit the famous churches of old Goa
12:15 pm: Visit an old Portuguese house; Sola Souto Maior for
tea/coffee and cookies; shopping at the emporium
02:00 pm: Reach the Zuri White Sands Resort for lunch followed by a beach stroll & activities 
05:30 pm: Depart from the Resort
06:30 pm: Return to The Golden Chariot
Dinner on board The Golden Chariot
07:10 pm: Depart for Bangalore

Day 8

Monday - Bangalore
08:00 am: Breakfast on board The Golden Chariot
11:00 am: Reach Yeswanthpur Railway Station
Farewell by the entire crew
Tour Ends.
